Solar energy is one of the most widely used renewable energy sources in the world. It works by converting sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells. Solar panels are commonly installed on rooftops and open fields. The main advantage of solar energy is that it produces clean electricity without emitting greenhouse gases during operation.
The French Revolution began in 1789 and was a period of radical political and social change in France. It led to the fall of the monarchy, the rise of republicanism, and significant changes in European politics. Key causes included economic hardship, inequality, and dissatisfaction with absolute monarchy.
